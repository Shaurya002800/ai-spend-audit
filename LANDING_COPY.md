## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no catch. SpendLens is a free tool by Credex. We make money when teams with large savings opportunities choose to buy discounted AI credits through Credex — but you get the audit either way.

**Q: Where does the pricing data come from?**
A: Every price is sourced from official vendor pricing pages and verified during submission week. Sources are cited in PRICING_DATA.md. We update pricing when vendors change their plans.

**Q: What does the audit actually check?**
A: For each tool you're on, it checks: (1) Are you on the right plan for your team size? (2) Is there a cheaper plan from the same vendor? (3) Is there a substantially cheaper alternative tool with similar capability for your use case? (4) Are you paying retail when discounted credits are available?

**Q: What's Credex?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real (20–30% off retail). SpendLens is their free tool for helping startups understand if those discounts would help them.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: Your audit is stored and associated with a shareable link. If you give us your email, we use it to send you the report and — if you're a high-savings case — have someone from Credex reach out. We don't sell data. The public share URL strips your email and company name.
