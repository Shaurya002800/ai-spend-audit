## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. A typical deal: a 20-person engineering team currently paying $800/mo retail for Cursor Business buys 12 months of credits at 25% off ‚Äî saving $200/mo, paying Credex $600/mo for credits worth $800/mo. Credex's gross margin on the discount arbitrage is roughly 10‚Äì15% of face value (the spread between their bulk acquisition cost and the discount they offer).

**Estimated deal value per converted customer:**
- Average team: 15 engineers, $500/mo AI spend ‚Üí $6,000/year retail
- Credex sells at 25% discount ‚Üí customer pays $4,500/year
- Credex buys credits at ~$3,750/year (estimates based on typical bulk pricing)
- Gross profit per customer: ~$750/year
- With 3-year LTV assumption: **~$2,250 LTV per customer**

Conservative estimate for a "high-savings" lead (the target): **$500‚Äì$2,500 LTV**.

## Conversion Rate Math

The funnel:

```
Landing page visitors ‚Üí audit completed ‚Üí email captured ‚Üí consultation booked ‚Üí credit purchase
     100%                   20‚Äì25%            25‚Äì30%              3‚Äì5%               40%
```

For 1,000 visitors:
- 200‚Äì250 audits completed
- 50‚Äì75 emails captured
- 6‚Äì12 consultations booked
- 2‚Äì5 credit purchases

At $750 gross profit/customer: **$1,500‚Äì$3,750 gross profit per 1,000 visitors**.

The "high savings" filter (>$500/mo savings) adds a pre-qualification layer. Of 250 audits, roughly 30‚Äì40% will show >$500/mo savings (based on the pricing gap between common enterprise plans and optimal plans). That's 75‚Äì100 "high savings" audits ‚Üí 8‚Äì12 consultations ‚Üí 3‚Äì5 purchases. Same math, but the consultation‚Üípurchase conversion is higher (~60%) because the lead is pre-qualified.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR / $750 average annual gross profit per customer = **1,333 paying customers**.

At a 40% consultation‚Üípurchase rate: need **3,333 consultations booked**.
At a 4% audit‚Üíconsultation rate: need **83,333 audits completed**.
At a 22% visitor‚Üíaudit rate: need **379,000 unique visitors** over 18 months (~21,000/month).

Is 21,000 visitors/month achievable in 18 months?

- Month 1‚Äì3: HN, Twitter, newsletters ‚Üí ~2,000/month
- Month 3‚Äì6: SEO from blog posts + branded search compound ‚Üí ~5,000/month
- Month 6‚Äì12: Vendor doc placements, referral loop, word-of-mouth ‚Üí ~10,000/month
- Month 12‚Äì18: If Credex invests $5k/month in content + LinkedIn ads ‚Üí ~20,000+/month

**It's plausible but requires everything to work**: the HN launch succeeds, SEO compounds, Credex leverages its vendor relationships for placement, and the audit‚Üíemail‚Üíconsultation funnel maintains conversion.

The biggest risk: the consultation booking rate. Most B2B tools see <2% booking rates from free tools. Getting to 4% requires either a very high-intent user (someone already evaluating Credex) or a strong in-product nudge (the "$500/mo savings" flag is that nudge).

**The $1M ARR scenario is achievable, but only if the consultation CTA is prominent and the sales team follows up within 24 hours.**
