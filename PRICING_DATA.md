## Notes

- Enterprise prices are estimates based on public analyst reports and community data where no official price is listed. Any recommendation involving enterprise plans is conservative and notes the estimate.
- API prices are spot prices as of verification date. Usage-based tools show no per-seat cost in the engine; the monthly spend field is the user's own reported bill.
- All prices in USD per user per month, billed monthly. Annual billing discounts exist for some plans but are not used in the engine (users report what they actually pay, which may be monthly or annual).
